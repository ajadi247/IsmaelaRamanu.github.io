About Me

As a recent Economics graduate, I'm energized by the potential of data to unlock hidden patterns and drive informed decision-making. Having a keen eye for trends and a passion for uncovering the "why" behind the numbers, I'm eager to embark on a career in the data space, ideally bridging the gap between economic principles and data-driven analysis.
While my economics background equipped me with robust analytical skills and a solid understanding of market forces, I recognize the growing importance of translating qualitative and quantitative insights into actionable data-driven strategies. That's why I've actively pursued opportunities to hone my data analysis skills, taking online courses in SQL, Python, and data visualization tools like Tableau.
I'm a quick learner with a hands-on approach, as evidenced by my Analysis of Hotel Prices Project. In this project, I successfully scraped data from bookings.com, cleaned it for analysis, and explored and visualized the insights discovered, demonstrating my ability to gather, clean, and manipulate data, perform statistical analysis, and create compelling visualizations.
Beyond technical skills, I possess strong communication and collaboration abilities that I have honed over the years by collaborating with teams to build projects as part of my program. I am confident presenting complex data insights to technical and non-technical audiences, ensuring clear understanding and actionable takeaways.
I'm excited to contribute my unique blend of economic understanding and data skills to a dynamic team. I'm eager to learn, adapt, and grow within a data-driven culture, and utilize my expertise to help solve problems for communities.
